What is the difference between Valentino and Mario Valentino bags?

If we are just looking at the bags, we can see that most Mario Valentino bags are made from leather for a stylish look and luxe feel. Valentino handbags are made from either calfskin or lambskin leather which are typically higher-quality leathers that guarantee a luxurious result.
